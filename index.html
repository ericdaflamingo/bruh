Different types of therapy

People with mental health conditions often find talk therapy (psychotherapy) very helpful. There are many different types of therapy such as:

  • Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy
  • Interpersonal Therapy
  • Family therapy
  • Psychodynamic Therapy

Suicidal thoughts and how to deal with them

The most important thing you can do if you're having suicidal thoughts, is to call a suicide hotline or contact trusted friends and family immediately.
